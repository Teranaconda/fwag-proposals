About Florida Windows & Glass

Florida Windows & Glass Inc. (FWAG) is a licensed and insured impact window and door installer serving Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We specialize in protecting Florida homes with code-compliant impact-resistant products.

We handle everything from measurement to permitting to final inspection — so you get a single point of contact from estimate to completion.

Our Process

1

Free In-Home Consultation

We measure every opening and discuss your needs, preferences, and budget.

2

Custom Estimate

You receive a detailed estimate with product specs, pricing, and installation scope. That's what you're looking at right now.

3

Permitting & Engineering

We pull all required permits and coordinate engineering drawings — you don't have to deal with the city.

4

Manufacturing

Your products are custom-built to your home's exact measurements. Typical lead time is 10-14 weeks.

5

Professional Installation

Our crew installs everything, hauls away old windows/doors, and cleans up.

6

Final Inspection

We schedule the county inspection and ensure everything passes code.
