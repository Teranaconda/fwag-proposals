About Florida Windows & Glass

We're a locally owned impact window and door company based in Miami Lakes, serving homeowners across Miami-Dade and Broward counties.

Our team handles everything from your free in-home estimate through permitting, professional installation, and final inspection. No subcontractors, no runaround — just one team from start to finish.

We specialize in hurricane-rated impact windows and doors that protect your family, reduce energy costs, lower insurance premiums, and increase your property value.

The FWAG Process

From estimate to installation — here's what to expect.

1

Free Estimate

A FWAG rep visits your home, measures every opening, and builds a detailed estimate tailored to your needs.

2

Order & Permit

We order your custom-manufactured windows and doors and handle all city/county permitting on your behalf.

3

Professional Install

Our in-house crew installs everything to code — no subcontractors. Most homes are completed in 1-2 days.

4

Final Inspection

A county inspector verifies the installation meets all building codes. You get your permit card and full warranty.
